- By combining contrasting colors in jewelry, a piece can transform from simple to extraordinary. Whether it’s the rich contrast of black onyx with silver or the vibrant clash of turquoise with gold, color contrast adds personality and excitement to every design.

Top 7 Color Contrasts




Gold and Sapphire Blue
The warm, rich tone of gold paired with the deep, vibrant blue of sapphires creates a luxurious and timeless contrast. This combination adds an air of sophistication and elegance to any jewelry piece, perfect for making a bold statement.
Emerald Green and Rose Gold
The deep green of emeralds paired with the soft warmth of rose gold creates a balanced contrast of nature’s richness and elegance. This pairing exudes a modern yet classic appeal, adding a refreshing pop of color with a subtle glow.
Black Onyx and Silver
The dark, sleek appearance of black onyx against the cool, reflective sheen of silver creates a striking, edgy contrast. This combination is perfect for bold, contemporary pieces that exude sophistication and power.
Pearl White and Platinum
The soft, lustrous shine of pearls paired with the cool, sleek elegance of platinum offers a subtle yet stunning contrast. This pairing is ideal for classic, understated pieces that have a refined, timeless beauty.
Ruby Red and White Diamond
The fiery intensity of ruby red combined with the sparkling brilliance of white diamonds creates an eye-catching contrast. The warmth of the ruby against the coolness of diamonds makes for an alluring and passionate combination, perfect for special occasions.
Turquoise and Silver
The bright, refreshing hue of turquoise paired with the cool, modern touch of silver creates a balanced and stylish contrast. This pairing offers a bohemian and contemporary look, perfect for casual yet chic jewelry.
Amethyst Purple and Yellow Gold
The rich, regal purple of amethyst stones contrasted with the bright, sunny hue of yellow gold creates a luxurious and vibrant effect. This combination is ideal for statement pieces that demand attention and add a sense of drama and glamour.
